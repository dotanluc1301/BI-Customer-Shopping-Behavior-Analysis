Negative Keywords: poor, bad, terrible, disappointing, worst, slow, broken,
                   defective, awful, hate, never, problem, issue, complaint

Net Sentiment Score (NPS-style):
NPS = (% Promoters [5-star]) - (% Detractors [1-2 star])
Range: -100 (all detractors) to +100 (all promoters)
- Excellent: >50
- Good: 30-50
- Fair: 0-30
- Poor: <0
